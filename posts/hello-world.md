## Why Write?

I've been a consumer of technical blogs for years. Blog posts from Cloudflare, Julia Evans, Martin Kleppmann, and countless others have shaped how I think about systems. At some point it felt right to start contributing back - even if my writing reaches only a handful of people.

Writing is also how I think. There's a clarity that comes from trying to explain something to an audience that doesn't share your context. You find the holes in your own understanding quickly.

## What This Blog Will Be

Mostly technical - networking, security, distributed systems, and infrastructure. Topics I work with daily and think about constantly.

But I also want to write about things adjacent to CS that I find fascinating:

- **Global catastrophic risks** - AI safety, biosecurity, how we build resilience into civilization at scale
- **Systems thinking** - how complex systems fail, recover, and evolve
- **The intersection of technology and policy** - where engineering decisions become governance decisions

I don't have a fixed publishing cadence. I'll write when I have something worth saying.

## A Note on Style

I'll aim for posts that are dense but not padded. No listicles. No "In this post, I will..." preamble. Just the thing itself.
